About Me


Jin Seok Park, Ph.D. Candidate in Economics, University of Southern California.

My research focuses on urban and labor economics, with particular interest in how housing markets shape entrepreneurship and economic dynamism. I employ applied microeconometric methods and leverage rich administrative and survey datasets to answer questions that inform policy and deepen our understanding of cities and labor markets.
